# SKILL: Modern Finance Operations (Rippling Strategy)

## 1. Skill Overview
This skill set focuses on leveraging AI for high-level financial planning, analysis, and operational efficiency, modeled after the strategies used by Rippling's finance team.

## 2. Core Competencies

### A. Benchmarking & Capital Allocation
*   **Goal:** Set realistic expectations and improve processes by comparing against peer performance.
*   **Key Metrics:**
    *   R&D Spend (incl. Stock-Based Comp) vs. Revenue Scale.
    *   SaaS Implementation Costs as % of ARR.
    *   Overhead as % of Revenue (Industry Specific).
*   **Prompt template:** "What are good benchmarks for [METRIC] for a company of my size in [INDUSTRY]?"

### B. Boilerplate Policy Creation & Audit
*   **Goal:** Control spending and eliminate the "cold start" problem for new governance documents.
*   **Capabilities:**
    *   **Loophole Detection:** Analyzing existing expense policies for exploitability.
    *   **Approval Efficiency:** Redesigning approval chains to reduce executive bottleneck.
    *   **Limit Setting:** Defining reasonable travel/meal limits based on company size/location.
*   **Prompt template:** "Where are the potential loopholes in this policy? How can I make approvals more efficient?"

### C. Competitive Deep Research
*   **Goal:** Comprehensive understanding of market positioning and competitor financials.
*   **Analysis Points:**
    *   Financial Performance (Growth, Profitability, Outlook).
    *   Product Portfolio & Mix.
    *   Market Positioning & Customer Segments.
    *   SWOT Analysis.
*   **Prompt template:** "Review [COMPETITOR]'s financial performance... and benchmark them against [MY COMPANY]."

### D. Technical Financial Execution
*   **Document Analysis:** Summarizing key terms of contracts or financial reports.
*   **Decision Support:** Presenting trade-offs for critical financial decisions (Buy vs. Build, Hire vs. Contract).
*   **Excel/Sheets Engineering:** Writing complex formulas for financial modeling.

## 3. Integration with Tools
*   **ChatGPT/Gemini:** The primary "Thinking Partner" for brainstorming and drafting.
*   **Rippling Spend (Concept):** Examples of automated enforcement (blocking out-of-policy spend, real-time reconciliation).
*   **Excel/Google Sheets:** The execution layer for models built with AI assistance.
